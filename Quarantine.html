Some city sugestions:

Learn to Code

Why not learn a different kind of language? Start learning to program and work on a small personal project and feel acomplished by a new skill.

Coding Ideas:

  • Make your personal Webpage
  • Make one small game
  • Make a blog about your opinions
  • Automate one daily online routine
  • Make a new mobile app
  • Prepare a automatic email reply
  • Make your own SoundBoard
